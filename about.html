About Us

English Learning Web is an innovative platform inspired by Duolingo, designed to make learning English fun and interactive. With a focus on community, game-based lessons, and a relaxing lo-fi aesthetic, we help users master English through engaging experiences.

Our Mission

We aim to create a supportive community where learners can practice English together, share progress, and enjoy a stress-free learning environment.

The Team

Our team consists of passionate educators and developers who believe in making language learning accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
